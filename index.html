The Science

SENSOR TECHNOLOGY
THE WORLD HAS
NEVER SEEN
IN A CONSUMER DEVICE

Three patented technologies, originally engineered for the most demanding environments on earth, now integrated into a single handheld platform. The sensor array detects compounds at parts-per-billion sensitivity, a level of precision that has never been available outside of a laboratory. Until now.

01
64-Sensor Nanomaterial Array

Sixty-four chemically distinct nanomaterials, each engineered to detect a specific class of volatile organic compounds. Cross-sensitivity architecture eliminates false positives. Detection at 2 to 5 parts per billion. Response time: seconds.

02
Differential Mobility Spectrometer

Separates volatile and non-volatile molecules in real time for multi-compound identification simultaneously. No processing delay. No lab required. The analytical capability of a full laboratory instrument, compressed into a handheld device.

03
Centrifugal Microfluidics

A miniaturized circular disk that handles saliva and blood analysis with full analyte coverage and blood cell counting. The same device. Expanded insight. No additional hardware, no additional friction.
